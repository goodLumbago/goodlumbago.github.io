Swingy Mr Thingy

Swingy Mr Thingy is a free arcade game where players control a character named Mr. Thingy, who swings, jumps, and navigates through various areas while avoiding obstacles such as poisonous ivy and cobwebs that can slow him down. The objective is to achieve the highest score possible by jumping between platforms and dodging these obstacles. The game features simple gameplay with rope swinging mechanic and a pixel art style graphics, appealing to players who enjoy skill-based challenges, also integrated with Google Play Services.
